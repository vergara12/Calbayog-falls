Welcome to the hidden gem of Samar, Philippines – Tabokno Falls

Nestled amidst the lush landscapes of Calbayog City, this off-the-beaten-path attraction is a must-visit for those seeking to immerse themselves in the wonders of nature. With its serene beauty and tranquil atmosphere, Tabokno Falls offers a captivating experience that will leave you in awe. Surrounded by verdant greenery, Tabokno Falls is a picturesque waterfall that showcases the natural beauty of Samar. Located in Barangay Caybago, just an hour’s drive from Calbayog City, it is easily accessible via a short trek through the village. The cascading waters and peaceful surroundings make it a paradise for adventure seekers and nature enthusiasts. Not only does Tabokno Falls provide a serene escape from the hustle and bustle of city life, but it also offers a range of outdoor activities for visitors to enjoy. Hiking enthusiasts can explore the nearby trails, while those interested in eco-tourism can immerse themselves in the unspoiled beauty of Samar. There is something for everyone amidst the natural wondes of Tabokno Falls. Calbayog City, where Tabokno Falls is located, is not only known for its natural attractions but also for its rich history and culture. Visitors can explore landmarks such as the Sts. Peter and Paul Cathedral and the Handumanan (Old Barracks Building), delving into the city’s past while appreciating the beauty of Tabokno Falls. If you’re planning a trip to Calbayog City to visit Tabokno Falls and other natural attractions in Samar, various accommodation options are available to suit every budget. With a seaport and airport in Calbayog City, it is easily accessible for travelers coming from different parts of the Philippines.
