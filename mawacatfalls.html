Here are some terms related to Mawacat Falls Slide in Calbayog City, Philippines:

Mawacat Slide:

A natural rock formation that's shaped like a spoon, inclines at a 45 degree angle, and is about 50 meters long. Water flows along the formation, and visitors can slide down a mossy lane from the top into a cool pool below.

Oquendo Poblacion:

A starting point for a boat ride to Barangay Mawacat.

Foot trail:

A shaded trail that leads from Barangay Mawacat to the Mawacat Slide. The walk takes about 15 minutes.

Mawacat Falls, located in Calbayog City, Samar, is a unique and thrilling natural attraction known for its natural rock slide feature. The falls are nestled within a dense forest, providing a serene and picturesque environment for visitors. What sets Mawacat Falls apart is its smooth, slanted rock formation, which serves as a natural slide for adventurous tourists. The water cascades gently over this slide into a refreshing pool, making it an exciting spot for recreation and relaxation. The surrounding area is rich in biodiversity, with lush vegetation and the tranquil sound of flowing water creating a peaceful ambiance. Accessing Mawacat Falls involves a trek through trails that showcase the beauty of Samar's wilderness. The journey itself is an adventure, as visitors traverse through rivers, forested paths, and rocky terrains. Mawacat Falls is ideal for family outings, group adventures, or solo escapes for those seeking both fun and nature's serenity. It highlights Calbayog City's reputation as the "City of Waterfalls," offering visitors a chance to experience the region's natural charm and exciting activities in one destination.

Location of Mawacat Falls:

Mawacat Falls is located in Barangay Mawacat, Calbayog City, Samar, Philippines. It is one of the area's notable natural attractions, famous for its unique rock slide feature.

How to Get to Mawacat Falls from Calbayog City Proper:

  • Travel to Barangay Mawacat:From Calbayog City proper, head to the transportation terminal or arrange a private ride.
  • Take a jeepney, habal-habal (motorbike taxi), or private vehicle to Barangay Mawacat. The journey typically takes 30–45 minutes, depending on road conditions and your mode of transport.

  • Trek to the Falls:

  • Upon reaching Barangay Mawacat, you’ll need to trek through trails to reach the falls.
  • The hike is around 20–30 minutes and involves navigating forested paths and some rocky areas.
  • The trail is relatively easy, but it’s always best to wear sturdy footwear and bring water.

  • Hire a Local Guide (Optional but Recommended): Local guides are available in Barangay Mawacat to assist you. They know the trail well and can make your trip safer and more enjoyable.

    • Travel Tips:

    Best Time to Visit: During the dry season (November to May), when the trail is more manageable and the falls are accessible.

    What to Bring: Comfortable trekking shoes, a waterproof bag, swimwear (if you plan to use the slide), snacks, and water.

    Coordinate with the Local Tourism Office: To ensure a smooth trip, contact the Calbayog City Tourism Office for updates on trail conditions and other guidelines.
