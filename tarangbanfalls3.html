CALBAYOG CITY, TARANGBAN FALLS

Tarangban Falls Travel Guide Calbayog City, Samar, Philippines

  • The beauty of Tarangban Falls lies in its cascading effects which stretch sideways. It’s the highest waterfall in Calbayog City and people have been admiring it for many years due to its serene location and magnetic appearance. It’s a huge waterfall and instead of being upright like many waterfalls are, you’ll notice that the rock formations where the water flows through are slanting so it adds character to it. Tarangban Falls is located in Barangay Tinaplacan in Calbayog City, Samar, Philippines. A trip to the falls if you’re staying at the town proper will take a couple of rides and trekking. You need to leave your hotel around 7:00 am to start your trip early. You must then ride a jeep or van which goes directly to Barangay Tinaplacan then register at the entrance and hire a tourist guide for a fee. You’ll go again on another journey from the highway to Bangon Falls then from Bangon Falls another 30 to 40 minutes of trekking to finally reach this wonderful destination. Don’t worry because it’s such a stunning place that you won’t mind all that travel just to get to the falls. You must bring your own foods during your trip because there are no eateries in the vicinity but be mindful of your trash and bring garbage bags with you and take it back and throw it properly. Visitors are advised to go home at 2:00 pm and are discouraged to visit during the rainy season. This is to keep everyone safe and also, to take care of Calbayog’s priced possession.

    • Activities

  • There aren’t many activities in Tarangban Waterfalls because it’s still underdeveloped but that’s the beauty of it, you get to swim to your heart’s content without bumping into many people. You can also take as many pictures as you like and relax at this unique piece of nature while it’s still untouched. Tourists guide will have you visiting Bangon Falls because it’s just near Tarangban Falls and it’s also a popular tourist destination in Calbayog.

    How to get there:

  • Book a flight from Manila to Calbayog via Cebu Pacific. If you’re staying at the city proper, you can take the route mentioned above so you can reach Tarangban Falls.
